Create Your Own Challenges

The 25 built-in cards are just the beginning. Design unlimited custom challenges with your own emoji icons and rules — perfect for inside jokes, themed nights, or house rules.

  • Unlimited custom challenges — add as many as you want with any emoji and rule you can dream up
  • Configurable frequency — set how often challenges appear (every 1, 2, 3, 4, or 5 rounds)
  • Toggle on/off — individually enable or disable any challenge, built-in or custom

How Party Mode Works

Same great gameplay, with one delightful twist each round.

1

Start a Party Mode Game

Select Party Mode when setting up your game. All your enabled challenge cards are loaded automatically.

2

A Challenge Card Appears

At the start of each round, a random challenge card is drawn. Everyone sees the rule — the explaining player must follow it.

3

Follow the Challenge

Describe as many words as you can within the time limit — while sticking to the challenge. Chaos, laughter, and glory await.
