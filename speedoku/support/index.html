Frequently Asked Questions

How does multiplayer work?

Create a lobby and share the code with friends, or join public matches. Everyone solves the same puzzle simultaneously. In Classic Mode, highest score wins. In Sprint Mode, first to finish wins (but one mistake and you're out!).

How do I earn XP and level up?

Complete puzzles to earn XP based on difficulty and performance. Daily challenges give bonus XP, and maintaining a streak multiplies your rewards. Level up to unlock new themes and climb the global leaderboards.

What's the difference between Classic and Sprint modes?

In Classic Mode, you earn points for correct answers with time bonuses - mistakes cost points but you keep playing. In Sprint Mode, it's pure speed: first to complete wins, but any mistake eliminates you immediately.

How do daily challenges work?

A new puzzle is available every day at midnight (your local time). Complete it to maintain your streak and earn bonus XP. Your solve time is ranked against all other players globally that day.

Can I play offline?

Yes! Single-player puzzles work offline. However, multiplayer matches, daily challenges, and leaderboard syncing require an internet connection.

How do I unlock premium themes?

Some themes (Ocean, Forest, Sunset, High Contrast) require Speedoku Pro. You can upgrade from the app settings. Pro also removes ads and gives you unlimited hints.
