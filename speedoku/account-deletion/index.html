Account Deletion Request

Request the deletion of your Speedoku account and all associated data.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information - display name, username, and profile photo
  • Your game statistics - scores, completion times, achievements, and streaks
  • Your social data - friends list, game invitations, and multiplayer history
  • Your cloud-synced data - all data stored on our servers

Note: Leaderboard entries will be anonymized. Local data on your device must be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
