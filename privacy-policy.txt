  ## Introduction                                    
                                                     
  Welcome to Speedoku ("we," "our," or "us"). This   
  Privacy Policy explains how we collect,            
  use, disclose, and safeguard your information when 
  you use our mobile application                     
  Speedoku (the "App"), available on iOS and Android 
  platforms.                                         
                                                     
  Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using
   the App, you agree to the collection              
  and use of information in accordance with this     
  policy.                                            
                                                     
  ---                                                
                                                     
  ## Information We Collect                          
                                                     
  ### Information You Provide                        
                                                     
  **Account Information**                            
  - **Anonymous Play**: You can use Speedoku without 
  creating an account. We generate an                
    anonymous identifier to save your progress.      
  - **Social Sign-In**: If you choose to sign in with
   Google or Apple, we receive your                  
    name, email address, and profile photo from these
   services.                                         
  - **Profile Information**: Display name and        
  username you choose to set in the App.             
                                                     
  **Social Features**                                
  - Friend requests and connections you initiate     
  - Game invitations you send to other players       
  - Your public leaderboard entries (username and    
  scores)                                            
                                                     
  ### Information Collected Automatically            
                                                     
  **Gameplay Data**                                  
  - Puzzle statistics: games played, completed, and  
  abandoned                                          
  - Performance metrics: scores, completion times,   
  mistakes, hints used                               
  - Achievement progress and unlocks                 
  - Daily challenge participation and streaks        
  - Multiplayer match history and results            
                                                     
  **Technical Information**                          
  - Device type and operating system version         
  - App version                                      
  - Crash reports and error logs                     
  - Online/offline status for multiplayer features   
  - General usage analytics                          
                                                     
  ### Information Stored Locally                     
                                                     
  The App stores the following data on your device:  
  - Saved games and puzzle progress                  
  - Game settings and preferences                    
  - Cached account information for quick sign-in     
  - Offline statistics (synced to cloud when online) 
                                                     
  ---                                                
                                                     
  ## How We Use Your Information                     
                                                     
  We use collected information to:                   
                                                     
  - **Provide Core Features**: Save your game        
  progress, track statistics, and enable             
    multiplayer functionality                        
  - **Enable Social Features**: Connect you with     
  friends, display leaderboards, and                 
    facilitate game invitations                      
  - **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns, fix 
  bugs, and enhance user experience                  
  - **Sync Data**: Back up your progress to the cloud
   so you can access it across devices               
  - **Maintain Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, 
  abuse, or unauthorized access

  ## Data Storage and Security                       
                                                     
  ### Where We Store Data                            
                                                     
  - **Cloud Data**: Stored on Google Cloud Platform  
  (Firebase) servers                                 
  - **Local Data**: Stored on your device using      
  encrypted SQLite database                          
                                                     
  ### Security Measures                              
                                                     
  We implement appropriate technical and             
  organizational measures to protect your data,      
  including:                                         
  - Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)          
  - Firebase App Check to verify authentic app       
  requests                                           
  - Firebase Security Rules to restrict data access  
  - Anonymous identifiers for non-authenticated users
                                                     
  ---                                                
                                                     
  ## Data Retention                                  
                                                     
  - **Account Data**: Retained as long as your       
  account is active                                  
  - **Gameplay Statistics**: Retained indefinitely to
   maintain your progress                            
  - **Multiplayer Room Data**: Deleted after match   
  completion (typically within 24 hours)             
  - **Crash Reports**: Retained for 90 days for      
  debugging purposes                                 
                                                     
  ### Account Deletion                               
                                                     
  You may request deletion of your account and       
  associated data by contacting us at                
  [EMAIL]. Upon deletion:                            
  - Your profile and cloud-stored data will be       
  permanently removed                                
  - Leaderboard entries will be anonymized           
  - Local data must be deleted by uninstalling the   
  App
